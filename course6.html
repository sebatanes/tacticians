Shield Utilization

Duration: 8 Hours

Cost: $150 per student *See Price Matrix for your agency size

Students: 12 Minimum, 24 Maximum

Restrictions: Law Enforcement, Military, Government Agencies and select private agencies only
Description: This course is designed to teach the operator proper deployment techniques of the ballistic shield. The techniques taught will cover vehicle deployment, ambush deployment, man down extrication, as well as approach techniques. Each drill will teach the student how to handle the shield while firing a pistol/rifle.

Facility Requirements:: A range is preferable; otherwise any large unobstructed outdoor area may be utilized.

Equipment Requirements:

  • Ear Protection
  • Eye Protection, SAE compliant wrap around glasses.
  • Range Training Uniform (Long Sleeve preferred)
  • Ballistic Protection (Soft Armor or Hard Plates are acceptable)
  • Hand Gun and Carbine or Shotgun (NO AMMO) Blue Guns are acceptable
