Stop The Bleed

(Certification Course)



Duration: 1 Hour

Cost: Cost: $10 per student All Law Enforcement Officers are Free

Students: 12 Minimum, no maximum.

Restrictions: No restrictions.
Description: Through our Stop the Bleed course, you’ll gain the ability to recognize life-threatening bleeding and intervene effectively. The person next to a bleeding victim may be the one who’s most likely to save him or her. Take the course and become empowered to make a life or death difference when a bleeding emergency occurs. Stop the Bleed. Save a Life!
Upon successful completion of this program a certification of successful completion will be issued from Stop The Bleed®. This certification has no expiration. This block of instruction will give the student information on proper tourniquet application and wound packing techniques along with proper patient assessment.

Facility Requirements: Classroom type of area with projector.

Equipment Requirements:

  • NONE
