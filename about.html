About Us

Tactician Trainers International (A 501(c)(3) nonprofit) was developed to provide advanced training to police officers and members of the military.

Police Departments throughout the United States have a large void in training. Training provided by the private sector is often too expensive for smaller agencies to afford and training provided by government entities often requires the officers to travel long distances for the training. This results in only a small fraction of officer being able to obtain advanced training.

The training procedures we use are time tested and operationally proven. We have acquired our initial training from some of the best in the business such as T.E.E.S., Tier 1 Group, Strategos International and Asymmetric Solutions.

Our instructors are current and former law enforcement and military operators who have honed their specialized skill sets during real world application.

For this nonprofit to exist and excel in the training of our nations protectors, it is dependent upon donations from our sponsors and government grants.

All training is free of charge to officer/departments of 50 or fewer commissioned officers
